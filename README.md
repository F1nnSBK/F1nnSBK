### Theoretical Framework

My research focuses on the geometry of high-dimensional data and the "Two-Language Problem" in production AI. To balance exploration and exploitation in real-time, I implement variants of the **Upper Confidence Bound (UCB)** algorithm:

$$A(t) = \arg\max_{a \in \mathcal{A}} \left( \hat{\mu}_a(t) + \alpha \sqrt{\frac{\ln t}{N_a(t)}} \right)$$

Currently exploring Manifold Learning to preserve structural integrity in embedding spaces, ensuring that latent representations are both computationally efficient and physically informed.
